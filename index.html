About Her

Alexa Mendoza was diagnosed with Leukemia, a type of cancer, a few months before she started High School. She is the first daughter and second child of Dave and Lori Mendoza. Her family friends and neighbors are Jennifer, Jack and Katie. Katie, however, becomes more than just a family friend and is Alexa's best friend.

She is a teenage girl who likes to cause trouble and pranks, in the hospital, school and at home. Despite this, she still has a kind heart and soul, she's always ready to help Katie and share anything she feels with her family.
